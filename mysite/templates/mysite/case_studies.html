OMS Application Development - US Investment Bank


ITCS Group appointed to develop significant components of the Bank's proprietary Order Management System, within the Equities division in Hong Kong. As part of the project, ITCS has developed DMA (Direct Market Access) functionality for several markets, crossing and several smaller Business requirements.

ITCS has provided the development for the launch of the OMS in new countries like India and major upgrades in Australia, Hong Kong, Japan and Singapore.

Datacenter build-out and relocation - American Investment Bank


Our client, one of the world's top 10 investment banks, engaged ITCS Group to assist their internal staff with the new datacenter build-out, relocation and decommissioning of their existing datacenter in the Tokyo area. The objective of the project was to build and fit out a 15,000 sq. ft. site including building construction, structured cabling, racks, network and telecommunications infrastructure, server infrastructure, and market data services. Approximately 1500 new servers were purchased and installed into the new datacenter.

ITCS Japan provided on-site consulting services covering project management, datacenter management, external vendor management and engineering support throughout the duration of the project.

Telecoms Expense Management - Global Investment Bank


Global Investment Bank appointed ITCS Group to provide Telecoms Expense Management / Business Analyst services for an Asia-Pacific wide telecommunications review programme. ITCS Group was appointed to help realise substantial telecoms costs savings and increased functional and operational efficiencies across the bank.

Following a review, a number of cost saving opportunities were identified and ITCS were further engaged to assist in the implementation of the programme that covered Voice and Data circuits, Trader Voice, Mobile, Audio and Video conferencing services.

Office set-up - Hong Kong Hedge Fund


ITCS Group was appointed to manage the office IT setup including the 6 trade desks. ITCS was responsible for IT design, procurement, project management and implementation. The project was successfully completed in 28 days.
